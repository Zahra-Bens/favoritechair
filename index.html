My favorite piece is Z Chair designed by Zaha Hadid !


"Z chair" by Zaha Hadid for sawaya & moroni
Image © Designboom

the design is a simple three-dimensional gesture that zigzags in space as part of the continued discourse between form and function, elegance and utility, differentiation and continuity. geometric abstractions inform the design's linear loop, articulated along its path in a language that alternates thin wire streams and large surfaces to provide both ergonomic affordances and inherent stability to the overall shape. the subtle play of contrasting angular corners and wide, smooth curves are a result of the calligraphic gestures of Hadid's two-dimensional works, a three-dimensional presentation of a controlled brush stroke on a canvas, the synthesis of an idea: the sketch.
